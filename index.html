ABOUT CHESSIE

Chessie has been making music inspired by trains and railroads for more than 20 years. Their music brings the parallels and intersections, connections and disruptions, and sounds and silences embodied in the railway environment to life. Motion, "noise," sound, harmony, chance, structure, gathering and dispersal all have a place in their process of composition and performance of their music.

Chessie has released music on Drop Beat, Plug Research, Lok-Musik, and a host of other lables that have made themed compilations. They also released a DVD with an accompanying CD that repackaged an art installation they built and performed with visual artist Colby Caldwell at the now shuttered Fusebox gallery.

Chessie is sustained by Stephen Gardner and Ben Bailes's enduring frienship and collaborative relationship. While they lead busy lives, Gardner and Bailes continue to maintain a studio filled with vintage and contemporary recording gear and a wide array of traditional and non-traditional instruments of all types: acoustic, electric, electronic, etc.
